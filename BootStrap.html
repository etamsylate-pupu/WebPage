Albums

Namanana

《Namanana》is Zhang Yixing's third personal music album released on October 19, 2018, which contains 22 songs and is produced by Zhang Yixing. It represents the process to pursue his dream . He hopes his dream will be like a rainforest with vitality. Zhang Yixing hopes to promote m-pop (mando pop Chinese pop music) to the whole world through the album "rain forest of dreams". In the album, Zhang Yixing cooperated with many musicians, including singer Andrew bazzi, producer rice n'peas, production team from the United States, Mars music, and 153 / joombas music group.

           

Lose Control

《Lose control》 is an EP released by Zhang Yixing on October 28, 2016, which includes 6 songs and produced by Zhang Yixing. He hopes that the whole world will lose control when hearing his music, so this EP is called lose control. The concept of EP is expressed through the song name. All song names are connected with one sentence: "when the world is out of control because of music, what do you want? I miss you so much tonight. Let's relax.The six songs in "lose control" EP have different styles, stronger rhythm than traditional Chinese music, and more global arrangement style. The whole EP style is diversified and highly completed, showing Zhang Yixing's musical talent and the control of the first producer. The main song "lose control" is an impressive Pop R & B song with sweet guitar as the main tune, which has both gentle and sexy style.

           

Sheep

《Sheep》 is the second album. It contains ten songs. Produced by Zhang Yixing, released on October 7, 2017The album name "sheep" not only has the meaning of sheep, but also comes from Zhang Yixing's nickname "little sheep". Zhang Yixing wants to give back the audience's support in this way. Zhang Yixing hopes to break the public's established impression of "sheep" (i.e. Zhang Yixing) as docile and lovely with this album, presenting Zhang Yixing's three-dimensional and multifaceted nature. In addition, "sheet" is a singular and plural homomorphic word, which means not forgetting the original idea
